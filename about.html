About This Archive

I’ve always loved the nostalgic texture of film photography. After trying a disposable film camera, I bought my first point-and-shoot and started documenting life with film.

However, as I shot more rolls, it became harder to find specific photos when everything was mixed together.

So I organize my photos by roll. Each roll has its own page with shooting notes and a short story—helping me remember what I captured and why, while also allowing you to understand the moments behind the images.

Equipment

Camera: Minolta Capios 115s · 35mm compact film camer

Film: Kodak (Gold 100 · Gold 200 · Ultramax 400)
