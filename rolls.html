🎞 EP01 — First Roll

Kodak Gold 100 · Aug–Sep 2025 · Hong Kong

My very first roll of film. This roll captured the days before the new semester began — saying goodbye to friends and settling into my new life in Hong Kong. I was extremely nervous while shooting it. I had seen many examples online of film photos failing, and this roll was actually an old expired film that came with the camera when I bought it. Because of that, every shot felt uncertain, but also exciting.

🎞 EP03 — Hailey in Hong Kong

Kodak Gold 200 · Dec 2025 · Hailey's visit

This roll records the visit of my friend Hailey. We were university classmates, but now we live far apart and rarely get the chance to meet. She came to Hong Kong for a short visit, and we spent three wonderful days together. We explored the city, tried lots of food, and even visited Disneyland. Because moments like this are rare, I wanted to capture as many memories as possible on film.
