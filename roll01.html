🎞 EP01 — First Roll

Kodak Gold 100 · Aug–Sep 2025 · Hong Kong

My very first roll of film. This roll captured the days before the new semester began — saying goodbye to friends and settling into my new life in Hong Kong.
I was extremely nervous while shooting it. I had seen many examples online of film photos failing, and this roll was actually an old expired film that came with the camera when I bought it. Because of that, every shot felt uncertain, but also exciting.
